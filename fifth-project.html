Welcome to Publikwerks

Home to Publikwerker

Web Developer and Fine Artist Extraordinaire

After a brief stint in the US Navy, Publikwerker began his education at Kansas University. Initially a Physics major, Publikwerker soon switched to Communication Design.

After years spent upon the endless sea traveling the world, the MidWest proved too confining, and longing for the ocean air, Publikwerker again set out Westward.

Ten years later, and over 1,500 miles away in Portland, Oregon, the Pacific Northwest College of Art graduated him with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Painting/Drawing.

For almost another decade, Publikwerker co-ran a commercial letterpress printing shop in S.E Portland. Now, he's taking his Fine Arts background and his Production experience and applying them to Web Development.

His talents are available for hire.
