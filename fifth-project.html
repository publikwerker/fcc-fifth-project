Welcome to Publikwerks

Home to Publikwerker

Web Developer and Fine Artist Extraordinaire

After a brief stint in the US Navy, Publikwerker began his education at Kansas University. Initially a Physics major, Publikwerker soon switched to Communication Design, and eventually triple majored in Graphic Design, Illustration, and Painting.

The MidWest proved too confining, so he set out Westward. Ten years later, and over 1,500 miles away in Portland, Oregon, the Pacific Northwest College of Art graduated him with a Painting/Drawing degree.

For almost another decade, Publikwerker ran a commercial printing shop in S.E Portland. Now, he's taking his Fine Art background and his Production experience and applying it to Web Development to offer the coveted Full Stack.
